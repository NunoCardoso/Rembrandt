&lt;s&gt;The third child of priest Constantin Deunovski and Dobra Georgieva .&lt;/s&gt;
&lt;s&gt;His grandfather on his mother &apos;s side was Atanas Georgiev ( <EM ID="x-139" CATEG="TEMPO" TIPO="TEMPO_CALEND" SUBTIPO="INTERVALO">1805-1865</EM> ), an active public figure in the struggle for the independence of the church during the Revival in Bulgarian nation ( <EM ID="x-142" CATEG="TEMPO" TIPO="TEMPO_CALEND" SUBTIPO="DATA">18-19</EM> century ) .&lt;/s&gt;

&lt;s&gt;In 1872 Peter Deunov was admitted to an elementary school and he graduated secondary school in <EM ID="x-150" CATEG="LOCAL" TIPO="HUMANO" SUBTIPO="DIVISAO" COMENT="90354(Varna)">Varna</EM> after the liberation of Bulgaria from the [ <EM ID="x-152" CATEG="NUMERO" TIPO="NUMERAL">47</EM> ] Ottoman Empire .&lt;/s&gt;
